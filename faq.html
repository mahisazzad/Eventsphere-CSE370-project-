Frequently Asked Questions

Attendees

Q: How do I buy tickets?

A: Explore events, select your favorite, and click “Buy Ticket”.

Q: Can I get a refund?

A: It depends on the organizer’s refund policy.

Organizers

Q: How do I create an event?

A: Login as an organizer and use the “Create Event” form.

Q: How are tickets managed?

A: You can set ticket types, pricing, and availability via the Ticket Settings page.
